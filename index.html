WHY SHOULD I TRUST YOU WITH MY RESEARCH/USER MANUAL/WEBSITE?

Born in the U.S. to a Venezuelan and Spanish family, I am a native speaker of both languages. Second-generation Spanish-speakers are very common in the U.S., but so many of them are unable to cultivate their family's language and maintain a certain level of native fluency. My parents did a great job sending me overseas to Venezuela and Spain and creating a home environment that helped me maintain that relationship with the Spanish language. By the American school system, I have an excellent command of the English language, and through my family and personal effort, my comprehension skills in Spanish are equal to native monolingual speakers.

I studied psychology and linguistics at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, have worked as project manager and consultant in nutrition labs, psychology labs, and even a pediatric office, and am now completing a master's program in translation studies in Barcelona. Because of these many opportunities I've been fortunate enough to find, I have the resources to cover a fairly wide range of topics.
